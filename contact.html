Our Foot Print

Velurea was established in the year 2010, headquartered at Mumbai. We are a 'Leading' and 'Premium' service provider in the Lifestyle Management industry. We are an entirely owned and self-operated chain of Lifestyle Managements and work across 12 major cities in the South Asia Pacific region and 25+ cities in India.

We are currently serving DQ Top 100 clients across the country. We also serve geographically divided companies across many industry verticals today. Our main goal is to expand our services to other areas as well and try to increase our global footprint.

We manage 'Work-Life Balance' and address key issues arising out of irregularities while managing work and life. Velurea has the capability of servicing 7500 requests monthly across 60 client sites, at 10 cities in the Country.
